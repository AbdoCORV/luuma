Our Story

Where Cairo's Heart
Meets The Nile

LUUMA sits on Abu El Feda Street in Zamalek, just beside Beit Ward, with one of Cairo's most breathtaking direct Nile views. From the first light of morning to the amber glow of late night, every table is a front-row seat to the river that defines this city.

The atmosphere is a careful balance: open-air elegance, warm materials, and the kind of unhurried hospitality that makes guests return. An all-day menu that moves from lavish breakfasts to exceptional evening fare — each dish handcrafted with seasonal ingredients and deep culinary intent.
